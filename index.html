When starting the test, you will be presented with a number sequence for 30 seconds. Make sure to memorize the sequence and remember it until the end of the whole test, you will be tasked to type it at the end.

Afterwards, you will be shown 3 different common household items for 2 minutes each. In the answer box below, input as many alternative uses of the object you can think of during the given time.

For example, if you are shown 'cabinet", your answers may be.

for non-switch group.

A bed to sleep on

Bookshelf to store texts and stories

A Playhouse used for kids to play around
